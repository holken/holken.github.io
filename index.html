I am a PhD student at RPL (Robotics, Perception and Learning) , in the social robotics group at KTH, Stockholm under the supervision of Iolanda Leite.

My research revolves around reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). My contributions revolve around making the process more sample-efficient and granular in order to achieve better alignment with the human.
